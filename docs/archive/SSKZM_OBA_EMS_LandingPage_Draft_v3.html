Trust Architecture
How your vote is protected
Structural anonymity — the system is designed so that no one can link your vote to your identity, not even the Returning Officer.
RO independence — from the day the Returning Officer is appointed, the EC has zero access to the election system.
Receipt token — every vote generates an anonymous receipt token so you can verify your vote was recorded after results are declared.
Data stays with OBA — all election data resides in the Association's own Google account. No vendor holds your data.
